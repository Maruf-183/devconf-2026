# AI Prompts Used — Placeholder / AI Challenge Section

## Section Idea: "48-Hour Hackathon Arena"

The placeholder section was replaced with a Hackathon Arena feature, since a 
developer conference audience is a natural fit for a live coding challenge 
alongside the talks and workshops.

## Prompts Used with Claude (Anthropic)

### 1. Ideation
"I need a creative section idea for a DevConf 2026 landing page to replace a 
placeholder. It should stay relevant to a developer conference theme. Suggest 
a unique idea that fits well with existing sections like Speakers and 
Pricing."

### 2. Content Structure
"Turn the Hackathon Arena idea into HTML content: a heading, a short 
description, three highlight cards (Open Theme, Live Mentorship, Real 
Prizes) with short descriptions, and three stat boxes (48 hours, 120+ teams, 
15 mentors), plus a call-to-action button."

### 3. Visual Styling
"Style this section using CSS3 only, no JavaScript or frameworks. Use a dark 
background with glassmorphism cards (semi-transparent background, backdrop 
blur), soft glowing gradients in the corners, gradient text for the stat 
numbers, and a subtle hover-lift animation on the cards. Highlight the middle 
card with a solid blue gradient to draw attention."

### 4. Refinement
"The section currently looks too flat and all cards blend into the same 
color. Improve the visual hierarchy so the highlighted card stands out and 
the section feels more premium and less plain."
